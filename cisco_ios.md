The Cisco IOS (Internetworking Operating System) is installed on all Cisco devices, regardless of size or type.

You may access the IOS on your in a variety of ways including:

* Console port
Connecting a serial cable to the Console port and channeling I/O with a serial monitor.

* SSH 
SSH may be used to connect to any device that has an IP address.
Before accessing the device through SSH, it must be setup.

* Telnet
Telnet, unlike SSH, is not secure.
Plain text authentication, passwords, and commands are used.
Instead, use SSH.
